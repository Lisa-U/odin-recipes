Vegan Hot Chocolate

Description

This vegan hot chocolate probably doesn't taste good, but it's easy to type out, so it has that going for it! I got it here!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa
  • 3 cups soy milk

Steps

  1. Bring water, sugar, and cocoa to a boil in a small saucepan. Pour in soy milk and stir until combined and heated through, about 3 minutes.
